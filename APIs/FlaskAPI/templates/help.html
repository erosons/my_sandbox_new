Using the Tracker

  • Dashboard Overview:
    Upon creating a profile, you'll find a comprehensive view of your selected courses and prerequisites.

  • Career Exploration:
    Navigate to the 'Career Exploration' section upon logging in as a first-time user and discover a wide range of career options.
    The Tracker matches these careers with courses, providing insights into your educational journey.

  • Course Planning:
    In the 'Course Planning' section, you can update your courses as you complete each semester and track the number of course units.
    Track your progress toward completing your requirements and stay organized throughout your academic journey.

  • Prerequisite Waiver Request:
    Use the 'Prerequisite Waiver Request' feature to request prerequisite waivers. Provide information about the course prerequisites and a brief explanation of why you believe a waiver is necessary.

  • Submit for Approval:
    Once the form is complete, your request is submitted for approval to your course advisor.

  • Check Status:
    Monitor the status of your request on your dashboard. You'll be notified once a decision is made.

Course Advisors - How It Works:

  • Logging In:
    Log in using your credentials to access the Advisor Dashboard.

  • Using the Tracker:
    Once logged in, you'll be directed to your personalized dashboard. Click on the "Advisor Features" tab.

  • Prerequisite Waiver Requests:
    Under "Prerequisite Waiver Approval," you'll find a list of pending student requests.

  • Review Requests:
    Click on a student's name to view their request details, including the course in question and the reason for the waiver.

  • Approval/Denial Options:
    After reviewing the request, you have the option to either approve or deny it. Provide additional comments if necessary.

  • Confirm Action:
    Once you've made a decision, confirm your action. The student will be notified of your choice.

  • Documentation:
    Keep track of approved and denied requests for your records. This can be valuable for future reference.
